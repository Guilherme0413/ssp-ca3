About

The Restaurant was founded in Dublin by Mr. Oliveira in 2017, after moving from Puglia. Our restaurant is inspired in the traditional southern italian trattorias. If you are looking for a new gastronomic experience, you are in the right place!

The Chef? Mr. Guilherme Oliveira himself

We are proud of our interiors and offspring, what makes us work hard everyday to deliver to our customers a real taste of Italia.
